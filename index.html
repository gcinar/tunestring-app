TuneString Instrument Tuner

TuneString is a simple and accurate tuner for guitar, bass, ukulele, and other instruments using real-time pitch detection.

Support: tunestring.app@gmail.com

Privacy Policy

TuneString Instrument Tuner does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Audio input from the microphone is processed locally on the device and is not recorded, stored, or transmitted.

If you have questions, contact: tunestring.app@gmail.com
